Fastest Furs Showcase

Thanks for Watching, Tiny Fox!

Whether you decoded our little message or one of your friends did and shared their wisdom, thanks so much for watching our event! TUNIC is a little indy game and a small speedrunnning community, and we appreciate you taking the time to check us out and vibe with our showcase.

Gun% Giveaway

If anyone runs, records, and submits a Gun% run during the showcase, we will gift them a Twitch sub to Fastest Furs! Use those emotes during the Fall Fest marathon in November!

We'll be on call to review and approve runs throughout the event, so give it a try! Be sure to check out Getting Started before the Gun% guide.

Rando Race

During the second half of our showcase, we will be racing the TUNIC Randomizer! Our community has selected a secret seed for the showcase.

If you would like to participate, download the randomizer and head over to RaceTime.GG to join the race! We will share seed and randomizer settings just before the race starts.
