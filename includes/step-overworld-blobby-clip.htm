Temple Door Clip

Use a large slime to ice-grapple into the Sealed Temple

  • Run SW of the Overworld Save and locate the larger blue slime.
  • Grapple the slime up to you, roll back 2-3x, grapple and repeat until at the Golden Door.
  • Position slime between the door and fox, take damage until critical.
  • Ice-beam the slime and grapple until you clip through the door.
