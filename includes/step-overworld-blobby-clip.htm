Temple Door Clip

Use a large slime to ice-grapple into the Sealed Temple

  1. Run SW of the Overworld save and locate the larger blue slime. If you are quick, it should always be the first enemy you can target in that corner.
  2. Grapple the slime up to you, roll back 2x, regain some stamina, and grapple the blob. Repeat until the fox's back is at the Golden Door.
  3. Walk around to position the slime between the door and fox and take fours hits (or until critical HP). Ice-beam the slime and grapple until you clip through the door.
