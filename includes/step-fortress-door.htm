Clip into Siege Arena

Knock back and ice-grapple through the golden door

  1. Enter the room and run to the top of the stairs.
  2. Hold target, fire 2 wand shots, 1 ice-beam, and grapple 2-3x.
NOTE: This is best done with digital (i.e., keyboard) movement, as you won't be able to target without the lantern and can easily miss wand shots.
