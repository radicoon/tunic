Resurrection Skip

Bomb out of the mandatory cutscene on first death

"Resurrection" is a mandatory cutscene that plays on your save's first death. It is long and unskippable with menuing or quitting out, and you cannot open the menu during this scene.

However, your inventory upon entering is still active. As long as you have a bomb equipped on your first death, you can use it here to death warp out of the scene.
