Boss: Scavenger (Grapple Off)

Push the Scavenger off the left side of the arena

  1. Make sure the speedrunner code is active. Equipment required: Orb, Dagger, Wand.
  2. If you have 12 or more mana: Run up and ice-grapple the Scav at max orb distance. Position yourself to the right of the Scav and blast it with two wand shots until the ice breaks.
  3. Otherwise, ice-beam the Scav from a distance and run to position yourself to the right of the Scav. Wait for it to thaw naturally.
  4. Immediately re-freeze the Scav during thier "ice-break" animation and grapple repeatedly (usually 3x) until the Scav is off the left arena edge.
  5. If done quickly and correctly, the Scav will pop into the death plane. Obtain the Blue Key, equip a bomb, and use it to death warp. Be sure to use the death animation to menu.
