Quarry Skip

Clip off the ledge and run directly to Ziggurat

  1. Make sure the speedrunner code is active—this is best done just after the second fuse. This requires the Orb, Dagger, Wand.
  2. Walk to the edge of the broken fuse north of the shrine, target the nearest Scavenger and grapple him to you. They should begin to fall into the miasma.
  3. Let them fall several frames and then ice grapple them. You should immediately fall into the lower Quarry.
  4. As you are falling, equip Effigy over Orb and move your cursor to Sword. When you regain control, run to the Ziggurat in a tight line around the debris.
  5. Immediately pause, quit, and continue upon entering the Ziggurat. This will reset your HP bar to its pre-miasma state.
