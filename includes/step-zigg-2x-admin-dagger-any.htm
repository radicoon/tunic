Admins Skip (Dagger)

Freeze both admins and power fuse before dying

  1. Run towards the admin on the right and freeze it with the dagger. Immediately roll toward and run a bit before freezing the second. Quickly activate the fuse.
  2. Run forward and aggro the voidlings (where the bridge will appear) and let the administrators attack them. Once the bridge is up, run through their wreckage, collect mana, and head toward the Scavenger.
To confront the Scavenger, you will need at least 3 mana before proceeding (ideally, more!).
