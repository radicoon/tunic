NW and SW Fuses

Grapple some frogs and birds

NW Fuse

  1. Walk down the left staircase and 180 behind it to the beach.
  2. Walk to the inner corner and aggro the frog with a wand shot. This timing is very tight; if you are slow here, the frog may walk away.
  3. Run along the beach behind the cliff until you are about to be visible again. Target and grapple the large frog down to the beach and continue.
  4. Run past the smaller frog, grapple up to the hook, run to and power the fuse.
  5. As soon as the fuse is active, move to the inside pocket of the brick wall and target/orb down to the lower hook. Immediately re-target the hook to the lower-right and grapple to it.

SW Fuse

  1. Run south toward the next fuse, hugging the lower side of the boardwalks and sand to avoid aggro-ing enemies.
  2. When you reach the mountain, run up the ramps to the fuse and activate. Pause, quit, and continue as soon as the power line lights up.
