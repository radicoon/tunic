Lower Ziggurat

Run through the ooze and ignore the baddies

  1. Run south and open the chest for bombs (5). Equip Orb over Sword while the chest is opening.
  2. Run around the catwalks, keeping a tight line. Ignore the turrets and voidlings, they should not interact with you.
  3. You will eventually reach a platform with a chest and turret. Immediately roll as you step onto the platform and keep running south. This will bait the voidling to attack and (hopefully) jam them at the exit of the platform behind you.
  4. Run right and open the chest for mana berries (3). Reverse course and run west and down past the second save. Skip both save points (unless you are doing safer strats).
