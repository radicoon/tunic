West Garden Skip

LS with Sword and run to Ice Dagger cave.

  1. Run past the Chompignom (affectionately named Terry) and run toward the water as he follows. When you reach the end of the water, you may pause and let 1-2 of their bites connect to damage down.
  2. Run right at the fork and underneath the overhead walkway, heading left, and across the planks to a small ladder. Ladder Storage off of the bottom of the letter to the right and run south under the main landing, diverting right to the Ice Dagger cave.
