Ziggurat Elevator Skip

Effigy to dismount the elevator, ice beam to fall

  1. Equipment required: Effigy, Dagger, Wand
  2. Activate the large elevator and walk to the south edge (bottom-right screen space). While waiting, type in the speedrunner code.
  3. As soon as you see a glimpse of the fox after the screen blacks out:
    1. Activate the effigy,
    2. shoot an ice beam up-left (north),
    3. and fire an ice beam while pressing no directions (neutral)
  4. This should all happen within frames of each other, one step immediately after the last. The fox should fall to the platform below and get up quickly.
