Ziggurat Elevator Skip

Effigy to dismount the elevator, ice beam to fall

  • Equipment required: Effigy, Dagger, Wand
  • Step on the elevator to activate and type the speedrunner code.
  • Move to the right-bottom edge of the elevator.
  • As soon as you see a glimpse of the fox after the screen blacks out:
    1. Activate the effigy
    2. Push your stick up-left and fire the ice beam
    3. Press no directions (neutral) and fire the ice beam 1-2x more
  • As soon as you land, fire an ice-rod to stand immediately and continue.
