Frog's Domain

Get tongued and obtain the orb

  1. Climb down the ladders, exit right to the hallway above the Orb room, and toss a firecracker over the 2nd railing If done right, the larger frog will aggro (but it's okay usually if more aggro).
  2. Walk up to the 1st railing and wedge into the upper corner, walking east (up-right). When the frog attempts to tongue you, walk very slightly to the right. If done correctly, the frog will pull you into the room.
  3. Run up to and acquire the orb. Quickly die, and make sure to equip a bomb in the death cutscene so you can skip Resurrection.
