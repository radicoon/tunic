To West Garden

Grab an effigy and use it to cancel a grapple

  1. Run north out of Atoll and into Overworld. Run up the right side and grapple across the gap. Roll left and approach the chest. Open the chest to obtain effigy (immediately equip it). Let the rudeling attack you once and run on.
  2. Head left and up the ramp to the dead-end chest. Grapple to the farther of the two tuning forks within reach and run north. Run to the stairs.
  3. Effigy Cancel:
    1. Run to the top of the stairs and aggro the turret.
    2. Immediately run to the bottom of the stairs and grapple the turret.
    3. When the fox is about halfway across the gap, activate the effigy to cancel the grapple and fall to the lower platform.
  4. As soon as you regain control, run into West Garden. Taking damage from the turret is preferable as long as it does not kill you.
