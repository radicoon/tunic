Admin Skip (Ladder Storage)

Use Ladder Storage to skip the Admin Tower.

  1. Approach the Admin Tower and ladder storage off of it with the sword.
  2. While in LS, run left of the tower as the camera turns to the back-left corner. After a second of leaving the catwalk area, start mashing attack with the sword.
  3. When the fairy chasing you readies a shot, quickly target the fairy (while still mashing).
  4. If positioned correctly, you will be hit with the bolt, be knocked out of Ladder Storage, and fall into a loading zone to Lower Ziggurat.
