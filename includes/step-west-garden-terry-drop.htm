Drop Terry

Avoid the chompin and get a free ice bomb

  1. Run past the Chompignom (affectionately named Terry) and run toward the water as he follows.
  2. Roll into the water at the last moment (so your roll extends your run into the water). As soon as you take a step around the corner, grapple Terry across the corner gap and into the water.
  3. Obtain a free ice bomb while walking in the water (since you can't roll or run).
  4. Menu as you roll out of the water to equip the ice bomb.
