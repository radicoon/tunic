Boss: The Librarian (Ice Bomb)

"Basketbomb" the Librarian to a death plane.

  1. Climb the ladders to the librarian and stop at the top of the first.
  2. Face south (down-right) and LS off the ladder up-left. Run left to the edge of the tower and cue a sword swipe. While swiping, hold up and throw the ice bomb.
  3. If done correctly, the bomb will explode in mid-air after a precise bounce and aggro the Librarian. Sit tight and wait for the Librarian to collide with the death plane. If he preps the seeking orb attack, you can move down the ladder enough to dodge it with good timing.
  4. Once the Librarian dies, climb back to the top of the tower and retrieve the Green Key. Throw a bomb to death warp back to Atoll.
