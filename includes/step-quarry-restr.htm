Quarry Run

Avoid Scavengers and enter the Ziggurat

Damage Down

  1. Aggro the nearest Scavenger with a wand shot. Quickly switch to Muffling Bell.
  2. Hold target to keep the sniper in view and take a sniper hit. Immediately pause, quit, and continue.

Quarry Run

  1. Avoid the scavengers following a specific walking path (see video).
  2. If you are running Any% or want safety mana berries, veer left of the shortcut and open the chest for Mana Berries (3).
  3. Run into and down the shortcut to the right of the stacked boxes.
  4. If you are running All Relics, bomb the hidden wall and obtain Lure (2).
  5. Make it to the shortcut and activate. While the animation is playing, equip Orb, Dagger, Wand.
  6. Descend one ladder, grapple the sniper to the right to a small ledge, then descend the one-way ladder and fall to the ground. Once you hit the ground, quickly swap to Orange Peril Ring.
  7. Run counter-clockwise through the remainder of Quarry and into the Ziggurat.
You can instead menu to Sword instead of Orb at the shortcut and not grapple the sniper to the south. When you fall and switch to Orange Peril Ring, he will shoot at you. It's fairly easy to dodge and save a couple seconds, but it's risky.

Ziggurat Entrance

  1. Immediately quit and continue to reset your maximum health. Run through the rooms and descend on the elevator. Breathe, stretch.
