Boss: Garden Knight

Stun-lock with careful combos and peril state

  1. Pop your Echo of Self and run north toward the Garden Knight arena.
  2. Aggro a rudeling and quickly menu: equip Orange Peril Ring, sword, bomb, and wand and ideally place your cursor over orb.
  3. By this time, you should be damaged down to peril state from the rudelings. Run north / NE to the ladder and enter the boss arena.

Boss Fight

  1. Try to position yourself directly in line with boss and open with two full combos (1-2-3).
  2. The boss will backpedal. Roll forward and do a short combo (1-2). This should break poise and stagger the boss.
  3. Repeat the first step (two full combos). If the boss backpedals, roll forward. Perform a short combo (1-2).
  4. One or two more hits should finish the boss. Run towards the exit while the camera focuses on the boss. Open the chest for 200 bits. If you have more than 1 HP, switch to Orb.
