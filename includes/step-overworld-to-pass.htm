Forbidden Pass

Enter the Forbidden Pass and Quarry, powering both fuses.

  1. Head west and grapple down to the tuning fork on the lower level. Run north, climb the ladder, and run into the Forbidden Pass.
  2. Grapple to the first fuse and power it. Head into Quarry and power the second fuse. While the fuse is lowering, use this time to menu to: Orb, Dagger, and Wand. Collect the mana.
  3. Head to the Quarry checkpoint but do not save.
