Kevin Clip / Elevator Skip

Wedge out of bounds and fall to Lower Ziggurat

  • Equipment required: Sword
  • Enter/exit the Ziggurat tower and pause, quit, continue.
  • Hold LT / target and walk to the catwalk corner on the right.
  • From below the corner, apply about 10% pressure to the analog stick and walk backwards (just barely right of straight up) toward the corner.
  • When the fox starts to vibrate, swing the sword to move the fox back below the corner and repeat.
  • Over a few attempts, the shaking should become more violent until you clip through the floor.
