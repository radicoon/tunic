Kevin Clip / Elevator Skip

Wedge out of bounds and fall to Lower Ziggurat

  1. Go inside the Ziggurat Tower. Exit and immediately quit and continue.
  2. Hold LT / target as the game resumes and walk to the catwalk corner on the right.
  3. From below the corner, apply about 10% pressure to the analog stick and walk backwards (just barely right of straight up) toward the corner. When the fox starts to vibrate, swing the sword to move the fox back below the corner and repeat.
  4. Over a few attempts, the shaking should become more violent until you clip through the floor and fall onto a load zone into Lower Ziggurat.
Note: if the fox's shaking becomes SO even that it never seems to change, simply save and continue to reset and try again.
