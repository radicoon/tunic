Frog's Domain

LS off the base and acquire the orb

  1. Climb down the ladders and Ladder Storage off the bottom ladder.
  2. Exit right to the hallway above the Orb room and run through the railing and into the room. Allow the custodian to hit with the AOE attack to knock the fox out of Ladder Storage.
  3. Run up to and acquire the orb. Quickly die, and make sure to equip stick, orb and bomb in the death cutscene.
