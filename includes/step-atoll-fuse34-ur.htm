NE and SE Fuses (LS)

Avoid the crabs with LS

NE Fuse (3)

  1. Walk down the right staircase and LS off of the ladder. Run up to the NE fuse and, while holding run, cue a sword swipe and a wand shot.
  2. The shot will bounce off the fuse and knock the fox out of LS. Immediately activate the fuse.

SE Fuse (4)

  1. As soon as the fuse lights, orb down to the hook to the south and walk south along the brick catwalk. Target and ice-grapple the south-most small crab and head SW.
  2. Run to the white tree and grapple to the closest hook. Immediately grapple across the water to the next hook. Run past the crabs. The larger crab can be a good way to damage down to critical HP.
  3. Run to the SE corner of Atoll, up a couple ladders, and activate the last fuse. Pause, quit, and continue as soon as the power line lights up.
