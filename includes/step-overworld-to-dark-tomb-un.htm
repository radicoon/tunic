To Dark Tomb

Grab an effigy and head north to the tomb

  1. Run north out of Atoll and into Overworld. Run up the right side and grapple across the gap. Roll left and approach the chest. Open the chest to obtain effigy for later.
  2. Continue to grapple northwest and past the active turret on the corner by West Garden. Target and grapple the Envoy enemy to drop them into the water.
  3. Run across the now-empty rail and grapple up to the tuning fork above the chest (you can reach it when you're about halfway across the rail). Head right/east and enter Dark Tomb.
