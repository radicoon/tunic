IRC Start

Use the "Ice Rod Cancel" to get a huge head start

  • As soon as the in-game timer starts, shoot an ice beam to cancel the opening camera pan.
  • Immediately begin running toward the first Overworld save.
  • The camera will pan up to meet you about halfway to the save point.
  • Save at the Overworld save.
There's no big trick to this—it just takes practice! You will likely get stuck on geometry off-camera a lot, it's okay!
